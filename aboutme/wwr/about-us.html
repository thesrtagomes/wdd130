Interesting Facts about Canoinha Gringa

Our Story

Meet the Rios, a Brazilian family with a heart as big as the Lone Star State. They struck it rich in the oil game, but their real wealth? Joy. The clan decided to spread that joy far and wide, so they poured their fortunes into a whitewater rafting company. Picture this: The Rios, living high and mighty, swapping oil rigs for river rafts. Now, they're on a mission to bring smiles to faces, thrill-seekers and families alike, ensuring that every ride down those rapids is a ticket to pure, unadulterated joy. It's a Brazil-sized promise, y'all, and they're making waves, literally.

Canoinha Gringa Golder River

Back in the day, folks were on a wild hunt for gold in the Golden River. Fast forward to today, it's become a prime spot for thrill-seekers on rafts. Tales go 'round that tackling those rapids serves up seven years of good ol' luck. So, you've got these adventurous souls not just ridin' the waves for kicks but chasin' after a deal with Lady Luck herself. That river's like a slick Texas deal: rich in history, tying the gold rush days to today's promise of luck and adventure for those gutsy enough to ride its liquid fortune.
